About Our University

Excellence in Education Since 1965

Northern Northampton University (NNU) is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing world-class education and fostering innovation. Established in 1965, we have grown to become one of the leading universities in the region, known for our academic excellence and research contributions.

Our campus spans over 200 acres, featuring state-of-the-art facilities, modern laboratories, and extensive libraries. We offer a diverse range of programs across various disciplines, ensuring our students receive a comprehensive education that prepares them for successful careers.

At NNU, we believe in nurturing talent, encouraging critical thinking, and promoting global citizenship. Our faculty comprises renowned academics and industry professionals who are committed to mentoring the next generation of leaders.
